Risk Factor

Medium Low Risk

Xience Alpine Everolimus Eluting Coronary Stent System is a drug coated stent made up of Cobalt Chromium(CoCr) a non-ferromagnetic metal. Nonclinical testing has demonstrated that the XIENCE Apine stent, in single and in overlapped configurations up to 71mm in Length (avg length per stent is 18mm) has Proven to be safe in the following condition.

Up to 3T

Can scan immediately after Placement

Spatial Gradient field of 2500 Guass/cm or less

Max whole Body Avg SAR of 2.0 w/kg for up to 15min for a given sequence.
